## 5Ô∏è‚É£ FEATURES / HIGHLIGHTS

### **A. Business Problem / Goal**

Banks deal with massive volumes of customer and financial data.  
This dashboard solves the core challenge of **quickly understanding portfolio performance**, including:

- Which segments bring the highest loan value?  
- How are deposits distributed across demographics?  
- Which customers are potential cross-selling targets?  
- Where are risks or growth opportunities?  

The goal is to provide a **single source of truth** for loan, deposit, and customer analytics.
